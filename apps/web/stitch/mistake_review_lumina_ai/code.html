Subject-Verb Agreement Writing Task 2 • 2h ago

"The list of items are on the table."

"The list of items is on the table."

colors_spark AI ANALYSIS

You were distracted by the plural noun "items" right before the verb. Always look back to the head noun "The list" which is singular.

Tense Consistency Speaking Part 1 • Yesterday

"I have gone to London last year and see many sights."

"I went to London last year and saw many sights."

colors_spark AI ANALYSIS

You switched from a perfective aspect to a present tense while describing a past event. Use simple past for specific time markers like "last year."

Collocation Reading Task • Oct 24

"The city did a fast growth over the decade."

"The city experienced a fast growth..."

colors_spark AI ANALYSIS

"Growth" collocates with "experience," "undergo," or "witness," but rarely with "do."
