Phase 1: Logic Breakdown

Why this is correct

The passage presents a paradox. While designers intended to project "egalitarian" values, the author notes that the result was perceived as "implicitly hostile."

The key phrase "negating the democratic ideals" indicates a direct contradiction. Therefore, the "physical manifestation" (the buildings) worked against the "social philosophy" (the ideals).

Phase 2: The Trap

Common Trap: The Literal Echo

Your choice was a Literal Trap. It repeated keywords found in the first paragraph ("transparency", "raw materials") but ignored the "However" pivot in the second paragraph. In high-level reading comprehension, the correct answer usually synthesizes the entire shift in tone rather than just repeating vocabulary from the text.

Expert Strategy

When you see pivot words like "However" or "Conversely," focus on the text that follows. The question almost always tests your understanding of the contrast, not the initial claim.
